Hello friend,

Since September 2022 I am a Ph.D. student at EPFL, where I study symbolic representation learning, reinforcement learning, and sequence-to-sequence modeling. Here’s my CV!

Before starting my Ph.D., I spent some time at SPOC, IST Austria, and CUHK where I studied more mathematically themed problems in machine learning.

Outside of work, I like bicycles, books or good writing of almost any kind, peanut butter, genuine conversations, indie songs, dogs, running, steadfastness, climbing, the earthy smell after rain, wholesome vegetrian food, embarking on adventures, and coffee.

I sometimes take photos from things that I find interesting. You can check them out on instagram or unsplash.
